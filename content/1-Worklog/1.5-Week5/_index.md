## Week 5 Objectives

- Complete the AWS lab series from Lab 07 to Lab 11.
- Study CloudFormation, CloudWatch, AWS Support, AWS CLI, S3, Backup Plan, VPC, and IAM.
- Practice deploying AWS resources using both Console and CLI.
- Strengthen knowledge of monitoring, automation, networking, permissions, and infrastructure management.
