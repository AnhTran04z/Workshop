## Week 1 Objectives

- Get familiar with the internship environment, team members, and the initial working process.
- Understand the overview of AWS Free Tier 2025 and how to use credits effectively.
- Compare the Free Plan and Paid Plan in AWS Free Tier 2025.
- Understand the strategy to receive the full $200 credit and the required tasks.
- Identify AWS services that are suitable for learning and services that should be used carefully to avoid unexpected costs.
- Get initial hands-on experience with AWS Console, AWS CLI, EC2, and S3.

## Detailed Lab 0 - AWS Free Tier 2025: Revolution

| Section | Content studied | Outcome |
| --- | --- | --- |
| AWS Free Tier 2025: Revolution | Study the changes in AWS Free Tier 2025 and understand the new credit model. | Understand the new AWS Free Tier model and its benefits for cloud learning. |
