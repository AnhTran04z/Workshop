### Needs Improvement

* **Professional knowledge and skills:** I need to continue strengthening my knowledge of Unity, C# programming, networking, and AWS services so that I can apply them more confidently and professionally in future projects.
* **Ability to learn:** I need to improve the way I organize new knowledge after learning, take clearer notes, and practice more so that new knowledge can become practical skills.
* **Proactiveness:** I need to be more proactive in proposing solutions, finding approaches earlier when facing technical issues, and preparing alternative plans during project development.
* **Professional conduct:** I need to further improve my professional working attitude, especially in communication, responding to feedback, and presenting problems when working with a team.
* **Problem-solving skills:** I need to improve my ability to analyze root causes, break problems into smaller parts, and compare different solutions before choosing the most suitable approach.
* **Overall:** Overall, I completed the main parts of the project, but I still need to improve stability, proactiveness, and technical depth to achieve better results in future projects.
